Our Mission

The Saint Maurus Catholic Church Youth Ministry exists to lead young people into a lifelong relationship with Jesus Christ and His Church. We strive to create a welcoming environment where youth can grow in faith, build authentic community, and discover their God-given purpose.

Through prayer, formation, service, and fellowship, we empower young disciples to live out their Catholic faith with joy and conviction in today's world.

Our History

Saint Maurus Catholic Church Youth Ministry was established in 1995 with just a handful of dedicated young people meeting in the church basement. Over the past 25+ years, our ministry has grown to become one of the most vibrant youth programs in the diocese.

We've witnessed countless young lives transformed through encounters with Christ, and many of our alumni have gone on to serve in various ministries within the Church.

What We Believe

As a Catholic youth ministry, we are committed to the teachings of the Catholic Church. We believe in:

The Holy Trinity

One God in three divine Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

The Sacraments

Especially the Eucharist as the source and summit of our faith.

The Church

As the Body of Christ, entrusted with continuing His mission in the world.

Catholic Doctrine

As Catholic Christians, we hold to the teachings of the Catholic Church as expressed in the Catechism of the Catholic Church and guided by the Magisterium.

Core Beliefs

The Creed

We profess the Nicene Creed each Sunday, affirming our belief in the fundamental truths of Christianity.

Sacred Scripture and Tradition

We believe that God's revelation comes to us through both Sacred Scripture and Sacred Tradition, guided by the teaching authority of the Church.

The Sacraments

We celebrate seven sacraments as efficacious signs of grace, instituted by Christ and entrusted to the Church.

Moral Life

We strive to live according to the teachings of Christ, especially as expressed in the Beatitudes and the Ten Commandments.

Prayer

We believe in the importance of both personal and communal prayer as essential to our relationship with God.

Youth-Specific Approach

While holding firm to Catholic doctrine, we present these timeless truths in ways that are accessible and relevant to young people today. Our approach emphasizes:

  • Authentic witness from youth leaders and peers
  • Practical application of faith to daily life
  • Open dialogue and questioning in a safe environment
  • Community support in living out one's faith

Welcome to Saint Maurus Youth Ministry!

We're excited that you're considering joining our community. Here's what you need to know as a newcomer.

What to Expect

Our youth gatherings are welcoming, engaging, and focused on helping you grow in your relationship with God. You'll find a mix of games, small group discussions, prayer, and teaching relevant to your life as a young Catholic.

When We Meet

High School: Sundays, 6:30-8:00 PM

Middle School: Wednesdays, 4:30-6:00 PM

Youth Mass: First Sunday of each month, 5:00 PM

What to Bring

Just bring yourself! We provide all materials. If you have a Bible, feel free to bring it, but we have extras if you don't. Wear comfortable clothes as we often have active games and activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be Catholic to attend? No, all are welcome regardless of religious background.

Is there a cost? Most events are free, though some special events may have a small fee.

Do my parents need to stay? No, but they're welcome to join our parent ministry meetings.

Next Steps

Get Connected

Ready to take the next step? Here's how you can get involved:

  1. Attend an event: Come to one of our weekly gatherings to see what we're about.
  2. Meet our team: Introduce yourself to our youth ministers and leadership team.
  3. Join a small group: Connect with peers for deeper discussion and friendship.
  4. Get on our list: Sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about events.

Common Questions

Who can participate in youth ministry?

Our youth ministry is open to all young people in grades 6-12. You don't need to be a member of Saint Maurus parish to participate - all are welcome!

What should I expect at a typical youth night?

A typical youth night includes icebreaker games, small group discussions, a teaching relevant to teen life, prayer, and often some snacks! We create a welcoming environment where you can be yourself and grow in faith.

Is there a cost to participate?

Most of our regular programming is free. Some special events like retreats or mission trips may have a cost, but we never want finances to be a barrier. Scholarships are available for those who need them.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just bring yourself! We provide all materials. If you have a Bible, you're welcome to bring it, but it's not required. Wear comfortable clothes as we often have active games.

How can my parents get involved?

We have a parent ministry that meets monthly and parents are always welcome to volunteer as chaperones, small group leaders, or help with events. Contact our youth minister to learn more about parent involvement opportunities.

What if I'm not Catholic?

You are absolutely welcome! Our youth ministry is open to everyone regardless of religious background. We'll share our Catholic faith with you, but there's no pressure to become Catholic. Come as you are!

Our Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as when you register for events, sign up for our newsletter, or submit prayer requests. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and other contact information.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to communicate with you about youth ministry activities, provide pastoral care, and improve our programs. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Information Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These cookies do not contain personally identifiable information.

Your Choices

You may opt out of receiving communications from us at any time by contacting us or using the unsubscribe link in our emails.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at youth@saintmaurus.org.
